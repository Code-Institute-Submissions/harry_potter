Places to Visit

Warner Bros. Studio Tour London, located just outside London and accessible by bus and train, is the place to experience the magic behind the films with original sets, costumes, and more.

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Orlando has sets, rides, including a Hogwarts Express train, and more with Universal Orlando resorts on-site.

At the Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Osaka you can drink Butterbeer, choose a wand at Ollivanders, and enjoy rides and see the sets.

Experience the films on a large screen with other Harry Potter fans as a live orchestra plays the score. Screenings can be found in cities all over the world (though they might be in the local language).

Connect with other fans, dress up as your favorite character, enjoy activities and discussions, and, sometimes, meet actors from the films.

Meet other fans in Dublin, dress up, enjoy games and activities, and much more

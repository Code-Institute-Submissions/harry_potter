Where to Shop

Just next to the actual Platform 9-3/4 at King's Cross Station in London, where you can have your photo taken with a trolley "vanishing" into the wall, is the Harry Potter Shop. There you can stock up on chocolate frogs, Hogwarts apparel, and many other gifts and souvenirs. If you can't make it to London the store ships worldwide. Both Heathrow and Gatwick Airports also have Harry Potter stores.

Both Amazon and Amazon UK have all the books and films of course, and they also have party supplies, Legos, wizard attire as well as muggle clothes, time turner necklaces, and much more.

Ebay has a great selection of party supplies, costumes, and sometimes hard-to-find items which may have been discontinued.

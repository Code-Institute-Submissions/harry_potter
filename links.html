All Things Harry Potter

A Fan Site

Pottermore.com - known as "the digital heart of the wizarding world" which J. K. Rowling herself contributes to
Harry Potter Wiki - an encyclopedic resource for the Harry Potter books including community pages and discussion boards
YouTube - contains all the movies to watch (for a fee) and a wealth of behind-the-scenes glimpses, background stories for many of the characters, and much more
Bloomsbury.com - includes a bookshop, resources for teachers and parents, and quizzes and games
JKRowling.com - learn more about the author behind it all
The Harry Potter Lexicon - in invaluable reference to the world of Harry Potter

Just next to the actual Platform 9-3/4 at King's Cross Station in London, where you can have your photo taken with a trolley vanishing into a wall, is the Harry Potter store, which has chocolate frogs and many other gifts and souvenirs. The store ships worldwide and both Heathrow and Gatwick Airports also have Harry Potter stores.

Both Amazon and Amazon UK have all the books and films of course, and they also have party supplies, Legos, wizard attire as well as muggle clothes, time-turner necklaces, and much more.

Ebay has party supplies, costumes, and sometimes hard-to-find items which may have been discontinued.

This student project is for my daughter and other Harry Potter fans everywhere. Please feel free to send suggestions for additional links, places, events, and other resources. Thank you!
